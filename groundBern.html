1:00 PM - 2:20 PM Tomino Before Gundam Yoshiyuki Tomino is one of the most significant figures in the history of anime. The creator of Mobile Suit Gundam and the Real Robot genre, he would also create on of the earliest Isekai series with Aura Battler Dunbine, and continues to work in the industry to this day with recent titles like the Gundam: Reconguista in G films. But, Tomino didn't simply dive into the industry with Gundam. With humble roots at Mushi Productions working on Astro Boy, even after finally earning the role of Chief Director on Triton of the Sea, it was a rocky road of successes and failures, bold steps forward and failed experiments, that would lead Tomino to the creation of his most enduring work, Mobile Suit Gundam. Ethan Halker
